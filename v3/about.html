The Void

Where we came from. Why we build.

Born in the Underground

Nowhere Craft wasn't started in a boardroom. It started in a garage, with a piece of scrap leather and a refusal to accept the disposable nature of modern goods.

We believe in the weight of things. The smell of vegetable-tanned leather. The cold touch of solid brass. We build for the outcasts, the riders, the artists who need gear that lasts as long as they do.

Our Materials

Full Grain Leather

We only use the top layer of the hide. Uncorrected. Unapologetic. It shows every scar and scratch, just like you.

Solid Brass

No plating to flake off. Our hardware is solid brass that will oxidize and develop a unique patina over time.

Waxed Canvas

Heavy duty, water-resistant, and tough as nails. The same stuff used by sailors and soldiers for centuries.

Hand Stitching

Machines are fast. Hands are better. We use the saddle stitch method for durability that machines can't match.

Tactile goods for the tactile world.
